Our History

        Suvino Co., Ltd. was founded in 1987. We have been exporting leather goods to Europe and Japan market since the beginning of the company for 21 years now, ranging from ladies handbag and wallets, men's bag, to pet accessories.

        Our production facilities comprises of all imported machines which can deliver high quality of workmanship with precision. Raw materials used in the production processes are both local and import depending on customers' requirements. Thus, with extensive experiences, we are considered to be one of the best leather goods manufacturers in Thailand.

        With more than 21 years of experiences. We assure of our high quality products that would satisfy every customer needs. If you have any inquiries, please do not hesitate to let us know.
